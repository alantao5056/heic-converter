## Description

⭐ **HEIC Batch Converter** is your ultimate solution for effortlessly converting High-Efficiency Image Container (HEIC) files to widely supported formats on Windows! Featuring a clean, modern, and user-friendly interface, this app makes managing and converting your photos a breeze. Whether you're a professional photographer or just organizing personal memories, our powerful batch processing ensures you get the job done quickly and efficiently.

**Key Benefits:**
*   ⚡ **Lightning Fast Batch Processing:** Convert hundreds of HEIC images simultaneously, saving you valuable time.
*   📸 **Multiple Output Formats:** Seamlessly convert your HEIC photos to popular formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, and BMP.
*   🎯 **Precision Quality Control:** Fine-tune your JPG output quality with an intuitive real-time slider to balance file size and image clarity.
*   📁 **Smart File Management:** Easily configure how to handle original files (Keep, Delete, or Move) and resolve naming conflicts automatically.
*   📈 **Clear Progress Tracking:** Monitor your conversion progress, success rates, and failure statistics directly within the app's clean workspace.
